CRUD for ministries

A campus is defined as the official government list of campuses in the country. A ministry happens on a campus, or several campuses, or no campus at all. So everything is attached to a ministry… (ministry should read “movement”…)

There is a heirachy of ministries, then campuses can be tied to ministries, but technically aren‘t part of the ministry heirachy
